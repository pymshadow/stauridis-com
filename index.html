a little about me

Panagiotis Stauridis

I'm a photographer & filmmaker based in Athens, drawn to natural light and genuine connection. My focus is romantic couples and modern portraits — capturing emotion in a way that feels real, chasing moments that aren't staged.

From intimate sessions to weddings, events and film, every project is approached with the same care.
